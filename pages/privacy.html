Introduction

BookVault ("we," "us," or "our") operates the BookVault website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our website and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We collect limited data to improve your reading experience. This may include your reading preferences, search history, and device information. We collect this information to provide personalized recommendations and improve our service quality.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • First name and last name (if you choose to provide)
  • Reading preferences and history
  • Device information

Use of Data

BookVault uses the collected data for various purposes: to provide and maintain our website, to notify you about changes, to allow you to use interactive features, to provide customer support, and to gather analysis to improve service quality.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to external websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
